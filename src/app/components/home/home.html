Recruit Unit x1

Be notified of work opportunities which match your specific criteria.

Smooth the initial communication between developers and recruiters through personalised forms.

Recruiters submit a job to a developer through a form which is checked against the developers criteria. Both parties then instantly see if the role passes or fails to meet the criteria.

Recruit Unit will save time and frustration for all involved and make the next step in the process to a new role more productive.

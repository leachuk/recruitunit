What Is Recruit Unit?

The initial communication between developers and recruiters can be difficult. Recruit Unit makes this smoother by allowing software developers to define specific, unit test like criteria for the role they want.

The recruiter job submissions are then checked against these rules. This allows both parties to instantly see if the role passes or fails to meet the criteria.

Recruit Unit will save time and frustration for all involved and make the next step in the process to a new role more productive.
